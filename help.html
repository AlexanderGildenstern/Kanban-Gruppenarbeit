AddTask

In the AddTask section you can create a new task. You can designate your task with the tag title. Choose your category, urgency and write a short description. Now specify the due date. At least select the person how solve the task and creat it.

Board

At the board you can see all the tasks which have been created. There are four fields where you can switch your task. If the task are finished or wrong you can delete it.

Backlog

In the backlog section you see an overview from all created tasks
